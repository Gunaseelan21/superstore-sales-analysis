# Superstore Sales Insights

## Overview
I worked on a retail sales dataset to understand how the business is performing across categories, regions, and customers.

## Sales Analysis
From the analysis, I observed that the Technology category generates the highest sales compared to other categories. Furniture and Office Supplies also contribute, but not as much as Technology.

## Profit Analysis
Even though Furniture has good sales, its profit is relatively low. This could be due to higher discounts or costs. The West region shows the highest profit among all regions.

## Regional Performance
The West and East regions are performing better in terms of profit, while Central and South regions are slightly lower.

## Customer Insights
A small group of customers contributes a large portion of total sales. Identifying and retaining these customers is important for business growth.

## Trend Analysis
Sales have increased over the years, especially after 2015, which indicates steady business growth.

## Conclusion
Based on the analysis, the business should focus more on high-performing categories like Technology, improve profitability in Furniture, and retain top customers to increase overall revenue.
